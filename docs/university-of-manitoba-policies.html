University of Manitoba Policies

As a student at the University of Manitoba (UM) you have rights and responsibilities. It is important for you to know what you can expect from the University as a student and to understand what the University expects from you. Become familiar with the University’s policies, procedures, and regulations specific to your faculty, college or school. The UM website’s governing documents is one important source of information, in particular the Academic and Students sections. The Student Advocacy office can also help you understand policies and procedures; find their information in the UM Learner supports section below.

Academic Calendar

The Academic Calendar is the University’s official publication containing course descriptions, program, and graduation requirements, as well as UM and faculty or school-specific rules, regulations, and policies. Familiarize yourself with the sections of university policies and procedures and general academic regulations.
