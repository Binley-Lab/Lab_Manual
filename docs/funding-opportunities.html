Funding Opportunities

Here you will find resources related to finding additional funding to support your research. Please note that this is not a comprehensive list, but one I am slowly compiling as I come across more sources of funding. If you have any suggestions for funding sources to add to this list, please create an issue. You are responsible for keeping track of funding deadlines and requirements, and if you require a letter of reference from me, please provide me with at least two weeks notice.
